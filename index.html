PROFESSIONAL PROFILE

Legal Practice, Judicial Experience and Academic Research

An interdisciplinary professional profile combining legal practice, prosecutorial activity and international academic research.

Francesco Ernesto Alessi Longa is an Italian Attorney admitted before the Supreme Court of Cassation, Honorary Prosecutor at the Public Prosecutor's Office of Milan and researcher specialising in Criminal Law, International Law and Sports Law.

His professional career combines legal advocacy, judicial functions, university teaching and scientific research, developing an interdisciplinary perspective that connects legal doctrine, institutional practice and academic scholarship.

His principal research interests include Criminal Law, International Law, Sports Law, Human Rights, Sports Governance, Artificial Intelligence, Cybercrime and comparative legal systems.

His academic activity includes scientific publications, editorial collaborations, peer review, conference presentations and research projects aimed at strengthening the dialogue between legal theory and professional practice.
