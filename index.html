ABOUT

Legal Practice, Judicial Experience and Academic Research

Francesco Ernesto Alessi Longa is an Italian Attorney admitted before the Supreme Court of Cassation, Honorary Prosecutor at the Public Prosecutor's Office of Milan and researcher in International Law and Sports Law.

His professional career combines more than two decades of legal practice with judicial activity, university teaching and scientific research. This interdisciplinary profile has led to collaborations with academic institutions, scientific journals and international research initiatives.

His principal research interests include Criminal Law, International Law, Sports Law, Human Rights, Lex Sportiva, Artificial Intelligence, Cybercrime and Sports Governance.

Through legal scholarship and professional practice, his work aims to strengthen the dialogue between legal theory, judicial practice and institutional development, promoting evidence-based legal research and international cooperation.
