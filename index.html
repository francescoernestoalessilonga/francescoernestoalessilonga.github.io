Biography

Legal Practice, Judicial Experience and Academic Research

Francesco Ernesto Alessi Longa is an Italian Attorney admitted before the Supreme Court of Cassation and Honorary Prosecutor serving at the Public Prosecutor's Office of Milan.

Alongside more than twenty-five years of legal practice, he has developed an international academic profile through research, university teaching, editorial activity and multidisciplinary collaboration in the fields of International Law, Criminal Law and Sports Law.

His work combines professional legal practice, judicial activity and academic research with a particular focus on sports governance, lex sportiva, comparative law, human rights, cybercrime and the legal implications of emerging technologies.
