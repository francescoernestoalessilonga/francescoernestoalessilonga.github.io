Biography

Legal Practice, Judicial Experience and Academic Research

Francesco Ernesto Alessi Longa is an Italian Attorney admitted before the Supreme Court of Cassation and Honorary Prosecutor serving at the Public Prosecutor's Office of Milan.

Alongside more than twenty-five years of legal practice, he has developed an international academic profile in International Law, Sports Law and Sport Science through teaching, research and editorial activity.

His research focuses on criminal law, international law, lex sportiva, sports governance, human rights, cybercrime and the relationship between law and emerging technologies.

He regularly collaborates with universities, scientific journals and international research groups, combining practical legal experience with interdisciplinary academic scholarship.
