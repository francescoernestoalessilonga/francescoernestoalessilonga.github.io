BIOGRAPHY

Professional Biography

A professional career developed at the intersection of legal practice, judicial service, academic research and sports governance.

Francesco Ernesto Alessi Longa is an Italian Attorney admitted before the Supreme Court of Cassation, Honorary Prosecutor at the Public Prosecutor's Office of Milan and researcher specialising in Criminal Law, International Law and Sports Law.

His legal career has combined professional advocacy with judicial responsibilities, allowing him to develop extensive experience in criminal justice, institutional practice and legal decision-making. Alongside legal practice, he has continuously pursued academic research with particular attention to comparative law, sports governance, international legal systems and the relationship between law and emerging technologies.

His scholarly work focuses on criminal law, international law, sports law, human rights, cybercrime, artificial intelligence and the evolution of transnational legal systems. These research interests are reflected in scientific publications, conference presentations and collaborations with academic institutions and international research projects.

In addition to his legal and academic activities, he has been actively involved in sports governance, technical education and institutional development, contributing to the promotion of integrity, safeguarding and evidence-based policy within sport.
