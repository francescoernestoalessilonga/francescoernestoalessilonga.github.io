Biography

Francesco Ernesto Alessi Longa

Attorney admitted before the Italian Supreme Court, Honorary Prosecutor and scholar whose professional career combines legal practice, judicial activity, academic teaching and international research.

Professional Profile

Francesco Ernesto Alessi Longa is an Italian attorney admitted before the Supreme Court of Cassation and Honorary Prosecutor serving at the Public Prosecutor's Office of Milan. His professional experience spans more than twenty-five years of legal practice, focusing primarily on criminal law, administrative law and sports law.

Alongside his legal career, he has developed an international academic profile through university teaching, scientific publishing and interdisciplinary research. His work focuses on international law, criminal law, sports governance, lex sportiva, human rights, cybercrime and the legal implications of artificial intelligence.

He has served as Adjunct Professor, researcher, editorial board member and peer reviewer for several international scientific journals, combining practical legal expertise with academic scholarship.

His research aims to bridge judicial practice and scientific inquiry, promoting evidence-based legal research and international academic cooperation.
