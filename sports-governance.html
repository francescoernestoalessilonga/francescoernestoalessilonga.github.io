Research and Governance

Academic research and institutional activity are closely connected through the study of sports governance, athlete protection, safeguarding policies, anti-doping regulation, disciplinary systems and international sports organizations.

Particular attention is devoted to the evolution of Lex Sportiva, international arbitration, ethical governance and regulatory harmonisation within the Olympic Movement.
