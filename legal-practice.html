Legal Practice

Attorney admitted before the Italian Supreme Court

More than twenty-five years of professional legal practice in criminal law, administrative law and sports law, combined with judicial experience and academic research.

Professional Profile

Founder and partner of Studio Legale Associato Alessi, Francesco Ernesto Alessi Longa has practiced law since 2000 and has been admitted before the Italian Supreme Court of Cassation since 2015.

His professional activity focuses on criminal litigation, administrative law, corporate criminal liability, sports law and legal advice to institutions, organisations and sports federations.

Alongside legal practice, he has developed expertise in disciplinary proceedings, judicial ethics, sports justice and international legal cooperation.
